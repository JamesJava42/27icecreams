## 75+ Years of the Same Philosophy

Gordon and Ernesteen McConnell started this creamery in 1949 with Channel Islands dairy and a hand-crank churner. The current owners have preserved the founding principle: use the best possible milk, churn it correctly, and add real fruit, not flavoring.

## The Eureka Lemon Story

Eureka lemons grow all over Southern California — they're the dominant lemon in California agriculture. McConnell's sources theirs from local growers and uses the full lemon: juice for brightness, zest for the aromatic punch. Combined with marionberries (a blackberry-raspberry hybrid developed at Oregon State but grown widely in California), the flavor is polarized: citrus tart forward, berry jam finish.
