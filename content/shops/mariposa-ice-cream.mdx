## North Park's Artisan Creamery

Mariposa opened in North Park — San Diego's most vibrant neighborhood for independent food businesses — and immediately became the go-to for adventurous scoops alongside classic California flavors.

## The Horchata Ice Cream

Horchata is a rice-based drink with Mexican roots, flavored with cinnamon and vanilla. Turning it into ice cream requires extracting the rice flavor from soaked and blended rice, combining it with whole milk and cream, and seasoning carefully with Ceylon cinnamon and real vanilla.

The result is a pale, creamy ice cream with a subtle grain quality — it somehow tastes light despite being rich dairy — with cinnamon warmth in the finish.

## The North Park Neighborhood

30th Street in North Park is a food destination in its own right: excellent coffee, independent restaurants, craft beer, natural wine. Mariposa fits into an afternoon of exploring the neighborhood's blocks perfectly.

## Seasonal and Mexican-Inspired Flavors

Beyond horchata, Mariposa rotates Mexican and Latin-inspired flavors: tamarind chili, arroz con leche, café de olla, fresh Jamaica (hibiscus). The menu changes with seasons and availability. Their vanilla-forward Mexican chocolate flavor is a perennial that anchors the menu year-round.
