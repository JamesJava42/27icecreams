## The East Bay Classic

Loard's has been a multi-generational ice cream tradition in the East Bay for over 70 years. Multiple locations serve Oakland, Pleasanton, Livermore, and surrounding communities. The menu is classic and deliberate — not trendy, not trying to be.

## Why Toasted Almond

Toasted almond is California's nut. Almonds are the state's dominant agricultural export, grown throughout the Central Valley. Loard's sources in-shell almonds and toasts them in-house before churning — this makes a significant difference in intensity. Pre-toasted commercial almond paste doesn't come close.

## The Neighborhood Ice Cream Shop Model

What Loard's represents is worth pausing on: the reliable neighborhood creamery that isn't famous, doesn't get press, and doesn't need to. It makes excellent ice cream using good ingredients, serves it in generous portions at fair prices, and has been doing so for seven decades. This is the foundation upon which the artisan scene was built.

## Family Celebrations

Loard's is where East Bay families go for birthday parties, graduation celebrations, and summer afternoons. The atmosphere is friendly and unhurried. No Instagram moment required.
