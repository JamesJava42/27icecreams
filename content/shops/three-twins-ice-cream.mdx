## The Organic Standard

Neal Gottlieb started Three Twins with a commitment to certified organic ingredients across the board — not just the dairy, but the sweeteners, mix-ins, and flavorings. He was one of the first ice cream makers to seek USDA organic certification for the entire product.

## Madagascar Bourbon Vanilla

Madagascar Bourbon vanilla (not related to the whiskey — "Bourbon" refers to the former name of the island Réunion where the vanilla curing technique originated) is the world's standard-setting vanilla variety. The beans are thick, oily, and loaded with vanillin and hundreds of secondary aromatic compounds.

Using real beans rather than extract means the vanilla flavor has depth, warmth, and complexity rather than the flat sweetness of artificial vanilla. In organic cream with no added flavors to hide behind, the difference is unmissable.

## Multiple Locations

Three Twins expanded from the San Rafael original to multiple Bay Area and California locations. Their pints are available at Whole Foods and specialty grocers, and they ship through Goldbelly — making them one of the most accessible California artisan ice cream brands for out-of-state visitors.

## The Flavor Beyond Vanilla

The lemon cookie and sea salted caramel are the other landmark flavors. The salted caramel demonstrates the same philosophy as the vanilla: one exceptional ingredient, treated correctly.
