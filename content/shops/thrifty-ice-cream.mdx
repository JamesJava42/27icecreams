## California's Most Democratic Ice Cream

Thrifty ice cream started being sold in Thrifty Drug stores (now Rite Aid) in 1940. The cylindrical scoop shape — created by a custom-made cylindrical scooper — is instantly recognizable to anyone who grew up in California.

## The Chocolate Malted Krunch

The flavor itself is not fancy. It is chocolate ice cream with malted milk powder in the base and pieces of malted milk balls. It is sweet, deeply chocolatey, and has a satisfying crunch. It is also extraordinarily good, which is why it has outlasted dozens of trends, acquisitions, and ownership changes.

## The Cultural Significance

Thrifty ice cream is present in hundreds of thousands of California childhood memories. It appears in music (Ice Cube's "It Was a Good Day"), film, and literature as shorthand for a specific kind of unpretentious Southern California happiness. The price has stayed low enough across its history that this was the ice cream available to everyone — which is exactly the point.

## Finding It

Look for Rite Aid pharmacies across California. The ice cream counter is usually near the pharmacy window. The cylindrical scoop machine is the tell. Ask for a double scoop — the geometry makes doubles particularly satisfying.
