## From Portland to California Icon

Kim and Tyler Malek started Salt & Straw with a food cart in Portland in 2011. By the time they opened their San Francisco location on Fillmore Street, food media had already declared them the most interesting ice cream company in the country.

The strawberry honey balsamic with black pepper became their signature because it captures everything they believe in: hyperlocal sourcing, unexpected ingredient combinations, and flavors that reward attention.

## The Seasonal Menu Philosophy

Salt & Straw releases new themed menus monthly. Thanksgiving brought Turkey & Gravy ice cream (real turkey drippings — it works, somehow). Summer features a beer and cheese collaboration. Winter has spiced holiday flavors. Every month is reason to visit again.

## The Pints Are Shippable

This is critical for the passive income story: Salt & Straw ships nationally through Goldbelly. For anyone outside California, this is how you get the real thing. Pint packs are excellent gifts.

## Multiple Bay Area Locations

Fillmore Street is the flagship, but the Hayes Valley location near the symphony is arguably better situated for a post-concert scoop. Cow Hollow has parking. All locations carry the same rotating menu.
